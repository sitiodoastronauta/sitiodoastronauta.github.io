Homemade Microscope


Description


The webcam microscope is an object to instrumentalize the science citizen, science based on the informed (a lot of times) voluntary of citizens that create, analyze and share dates and results. The citizen science may be applied for all of us, in any place and any moment. The workshop happens in two different moments, first the participants will be oriented to assemble their own microscope through the disassembly and inversion of the webcans lens. In the second moment the participants will be invited to collect, treat and analyze the samples.
